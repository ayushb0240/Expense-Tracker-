About Avra

Avra is a smart and simple digital platform to manage your income, expenses, and budgeting with ease. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a small business owner, Avra empowers you to take control of your finances.

Our intuitive dashboard, interactive charts, and detailed reporting tools allow you to visualize and track your financial habits in real-time. With secure data storage and easy access on all devices, Avra brings you one step closer to financial clarity.

  • ✔ Track income and expenses seamlessly
  • ✔ Generate visual insights with interactive charts
  • ✔ Export reports in Excel format
  • ✔ Categorize transactions for better management
  • ✔ Mobile-first responsive design

Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Avra. Find answers below or reach out directly to us.

📌 How do I add a new expense?

Click the "+" button on your dashboard and fill in the details.

📌 Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export data as CSV or PDF from the Reports tab.

📌 How secure is my financial data?

We use 256-bit SSL encryption to keep your data completely secure.

📌 How do I reset my password?

📌 Can I use Avra offline?

Currently, Avra works online only. Offline mode is coming soon!
